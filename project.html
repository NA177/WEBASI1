Project2

Smart Attendance System

With the development of technology, the newest attendance verifying method was basically a device which recognizes the fingerprint of the employees. This method has its ups and downs, but the basic issue about this verifying method is the crowding around it when work hours begin, which results sometimes in a wasted time, and in time of diseases it's not recommended to touch anything that has been touched by a lot of people. Therefore, the technology of face recognition would solve it all, it would save time, and it will result in people keeping the recommended distance when needed. The company must take a photo of every employee to store in the DB, and keep cameras which are connected to the software, when an employee arrives the system would recognize them, and it will put the status for the employee for that day (attendant).
